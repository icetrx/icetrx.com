Biography

Musa Mulenga also known as Icetrx is a music producer, beat maker / artist from Zambia born on 9th April 1995. Born and bred on the Copperbelt province in the city of Kitwe Icetrx completed his secondary education at Mukuba Secondary School. He further went on to pursue a diploma in Art (art teachers diploma) at Evelyn Hone college. The love for music has been there from a tender age. Icetrx began his journey in music with his childhood friends Edwin Mulongoti popularly known as Mr1000, Shaka boi bill, Komplex, Bay Dox, Lk, and Njasmith K . Icetrx and his friends formed a group called YGA which made a couple of songs although they didn’t do much so the group kind of broke down. years later,He joined forces with his former class mate Emmanuel Bwalya (pro), Ockley and and his fellow music producer Victorious to create a record label known as RadioAuthentic music which is getting bigger and better everyday. Icetrx has worked with a lot of artists such as , Bill , Komplex, Mr1000, Pro, Ockley ,S.O.K, Tosh young stunner, .Jemax, flyboy ,Blakfab, fresh, Killswiitch, T’WAYNE, just to mention a few. In 2019 , He has produced some hit songs like "Impiya teshimpanga" by flyboy, king Jeff and Jemax, kandace kakumeka, "Chikondi" by Ockley , he also co-produced Ockleys mixtape and recently just produced another mixtape by Killswiitch.
