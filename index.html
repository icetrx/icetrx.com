Biography

Musa Mulenga also known as Icetrx is a music producer, beat maker / artist from Zambia born on 9th April 1995. Born and bred on the Copperbelt province in the city of Kitwe Icetrx completed his secondary education at Mukuba Secondary School. He further went on to pursue a diploma in Art (art teachers diploma) at Evelyn Hone college. The love for music has been there from a tender age. Icetrx began his journey in music with his childhood friends Edwin Mulongoti popularly known as Mr1000, Shaka boi bill, Komplex, Bay Dox, Lk, and Njasmith K .
